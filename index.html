Cars and Motorcycles

One of the hobby's I have always been fascinated by since I was little is cars. This includes but is not limited to: driving a car or other motorized vehicle, watching other people drive them, and modifying them. Many people are able to turn this hobby into a career, whether it be as a mechanic, a youtuber, or even a formula drift driver. There are many paths to go with this hobby, both casual and serious.

How to get Started

If you are interested in getting started in the car scene, it is relatively easy and you will have a lot of fun learning about everything. Some things you can do to start your journey include:

  • Watch videos to see what you like about cars
  • Attend car shows/car meets (many are free)
  • Learn how to do maintenance on your car on your own

Careers in the Car Scene

Cars/Motorized Vehicles are utilized in many parts of life, so it is no surprise that it has been turned into a career for many people. These careers include but are not limited to: NASCAR Driver, Formula 1 Driver, Formula Drift Driver, MotoGP Drivers, and Youtubers. Below is the average pay for the jobs listed above.

Job Average Pay(USD, Yearly)
NASCAR Driver $103,858
Formula 1 Driver Varies; top drivers are making over $20 mil
Formula Drift Driver $27,880
MotoGP Driver Varies; top drivers are making over $10 mil
Youtuber Varies

My Proudest Accomplishment

Though I haven't talked about it much, when I mention the car scene, I am also indirectly including the motorcycle scene as they go hand in hand. That being said, my proudest accomplishment is being able to ride a motorcycle. I first started riding in the summer of 2019 and haven't regretted it since. The pure adrenaline I get from riding is unmatched and I wouldn't trade it for anything.
